Chicken-Biryani Certainly! Here's the preparation steps for Chicken-Biryani presented in a bulleted list: Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  • Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices, and let it sit for 30 minutes.
  • Soak basmati rice for 30 minutes, then drain.
  • Heat oil, add whole spices and sauté.
  • Add onions and cook until golden brown.
  • Add marinated chicken and cook until done.
  • Layer half-cooked rice over the chicken.
  • Sprinkle saffron milk, ghee, mint, and coriander leaves.
  • Cover and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes.
  • Fluff the biryani gently and serve hot.
Enjoy your homemade Chicken Biriyani!

Mtton Biryani Certainly! Here's the preparation steps for Mtton Biryani presented in a bulleted list: Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  • Marinate mutton with yogurt, spices, and let sit for 1-2 hours.
  • Soak basmati rice for 30 minutes, then drain.
  • Heat oil, add whole spices and sauté.
  • Add onions and cook until golden brown.
  • Add marinated mutton and cook until browned.
  • Add tomatoes and cook until softened.
  • Cook mutton until tender (use pressure cooker if needed).
  • Boil rice until half-cooked, then drain.
  • Layer rice over mutton.
  • Sprinkle saffron milk, ghee, mint, and coriander.
  • Cover and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes.
  • Fluff biryani gently and serve hot.
Enjoy your homemade Mtton Biryani!

Carrot Halwa Certainly! Here's the preparation steps for Carrot Halwa presented in a bulleted list: Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
